Ian Ashley Murdock

Founder of the Debian project

Life and career

  • 1973 - Born in Konstanz, West Germany, on April 28
  • 1975 - Returned to the United States in 1975
  • 1991 - Graduated from Harrison High School in 1991
  • 1993 - While a college student, founded the Debian project in August 1993
  • 1994 - Wrote the Debian Manifesto in January
  • 1996 - Earned his bachelor's degree in computer science from Purdue
  • 2006 - Was appointed Chief Technology Officer of the Free Standards Group and elected chair of the Linux Standard Base workgroup
  • 2007 - Left the Linux Foundation to join Sun Microsystems in March 2007 to lead Project Indiana
  • From March 2007 to February 2010 - Was Vice President of Emerging Platforms at Sun until the company merged with Oracle and he resigned his position with the company.
  • From 2011 until 2015 - Was Vice President of Platform and Developer Community at Salesforce Marketing Cloud
  • November 2015 until his death - Worked for Docker, Inc
